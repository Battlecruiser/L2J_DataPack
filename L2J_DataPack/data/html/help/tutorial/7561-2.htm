How to Fish:

A fishing spot can be wherever there is a large enough concentration of fish to make it worthwhile for people to go fishing.
If the water is over your head, you can assume that it has fish in it, but they are also occasionally found in shallow waters, such as the hot springs area in northern Goddard.
Each spot has its own unique type of fish. The first step to becoming a skillful fisherman is to learn how to find a fishing spot that has the type of fish you like.
Once you find a good fishing spot, you must be careful not to fish too long there! The fish eventually learn to avoid your bait.
When the fish stop biting, try another place.
