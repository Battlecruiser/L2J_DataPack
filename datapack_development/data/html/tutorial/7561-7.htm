How to Fish:

The bigfishshot was created by the Black Anvil Guild at the request of Amfitley, the founder of the Fishermen's Guild. Basically, think of it as a soulshot for fishing.
The bigfishshot enhances reeling and pumping. If these techniques are improperly used, the load on the fishing rod and the distance your fish can travel will double. So the bigfishshot definitely could have some disadvantages.
However, if it's used correctly it can make up for any mistake you make. Mastering bigfishshot is the final stage to becoming an expert fisherman.
