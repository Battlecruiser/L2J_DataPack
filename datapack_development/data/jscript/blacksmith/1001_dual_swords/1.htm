Naturallly, in order to create a dual sword, you'll need the two swords that are to be combined.
And of course, I don't work for free...
As you probably know, dual swords must be perfectly balanced.
Only swords of identical grade may be combined.
I am only able to work on weapons up to C grade. If you wish to combine weapons of a higher grade, you'll need to take them to the Town of Aden...
Remember, enchanted weapons use their magic once they are combined with another sword.
Ah yes, one more thing... Weapons that have had a skill added may not be combined.
